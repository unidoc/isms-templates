# 9.3: Management review

Top management shall review the EMS at planned intervals.

## Inputs

Status of previous actions, changes in issues/interested parties/aspects/obligations, extent objectives achieved, environmental performance including trends, adequacy of resources, relevant communications, improvement opportunities.
