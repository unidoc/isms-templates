# 8.1: Operational planning and control

Plan, implement, control, and maintain processes needed to meet EMS requirements and address risks/opportunities, including:

- Establishing operating criteria
- Implementing control of processes in accordance with criteria
- Controlling planned changes and reviewing unintended changes
- Ensuring outsourced processes are controlled
