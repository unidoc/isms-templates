# 9.3: Management review

Top management shall review the QMS at planned intervals.

## Inputs

Status of actions from previous reviews, changes in external/internal issues, QMS performance, adequacy of resources, effectiveness of actions for risks, improvement opportunities.

## Outputs

Decisions and actions related to improvement opportunities, changes to QMS, resource needs.
