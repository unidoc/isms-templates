# Hierarchy of controls

Apply the hierarchy of controls when eliminating hazards and reducing OH&S risks: (1) eliminate the hazard, (2) substitute with less hazardous processes/materials/equipment, (3) use engineering controls and reorganisation of work, (4) use administrative controls including training, (5) use adequate personal protective equipment.
