# 7.3: Awareness

> TODO: Workers shall be made aware of the OH&S policy, their contribution to the effectiveness of the OH&S management system, implications of non-conformance, incidents and outcomes relevant to them, and their right to remove themselves from work situations they consider present an imminent and serious danger.
