# 6.1: Actions to address risks and opportunities

## 6.1.1 General

Determine the risks and opportunities that need to be addressed to give assurance that the OH&S management system can achieve its intended outcomes, prevent or reduce undesired effects, and achieve continual improvement.

## 6.1.2 Hazard identification and assessment of risks and opportunities

Establish, implement, and maintain processes for ongoing and proactive hazard identification and assessment of OH&S risks and other risks.

## 6.1.3 Determination of legal requirements and other requirements

Determine and have access to up-to-date legal requirements and other requirements applicable to OH&S hazards, risks, and the management system.

## 6.1.4 Planning action

Plan actions to address significant risks, opportunities, legal and other requirements, and emergency situations. Integrate the actions into the OH&S management system processes and evaluate their effectiveness.
