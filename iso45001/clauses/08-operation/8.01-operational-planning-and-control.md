# 8.1: Operational planning and control

## 8.1.1 General

Plan, implement, control, and maintain the processes needed to meet OH&S management system requirements and to implement the actions determined in clause 6.

## 8.1.2 Eliminating hazards and reducing OH&S risks

Establish processes for elimination of hazards and reduction of OH&S risks using the hierarchy of controls: (1) eliminate, (2) substitute, (3) engineering controls / reorganisation of work, (4) administrative controls / training, (5) personal protective equipment.

## 8.1.3 Management of change

Establish processes for implementation and control of planned temporary and permanent changes that impact OH&S performance (new products/services/processes, changes to work, changes in legal/other requirements, changes in knowledge of hazards/risks, developments in OH&S knowledge and technology).

## 8.1.4 Procurement

Establish, implement, and maintain processes to control procurement of products and services. Coordinate procurement, including contractors and outsourced functions, with OH&S management system requirements.
