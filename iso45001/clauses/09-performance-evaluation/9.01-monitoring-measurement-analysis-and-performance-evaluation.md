# 9.1: Monitoring, measurement, analysis and performance evaluation

## 9.1.1 General

Establish processes for monitoring, measurement, analysis, and performance evaluation. Determine what, methods, when, when evaluated.

## 9.1.2 Evaluation of compliance

Establish, implement, and maintain processes for evaluating fulfilment of legal and other requirements. Determine frequency and methods.
