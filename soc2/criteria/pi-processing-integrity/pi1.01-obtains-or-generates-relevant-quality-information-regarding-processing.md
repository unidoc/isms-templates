# PI1.1: Quality information regarding processing

## Purpose

The entity obtains or generates, uses, and communicates relevant, quality information
regarding the objectives related to processing, including definitions of data processed
and product and service specifications, to support the use of products and services.

## Description

The organization defines data processing specifications, input/output requirements, and
quality criteria for system processing. Users are provided with information needed to
understand how data is processed and what outputs to expect. Processing specifications
are documented and maintained.

## Key Considerations

- Data processing specifications are documented
- Input validation requirements are defined
- Expected outputs and quality criteria are specified
- Processing documentation is accessible to relevant stakeholders
- Specifications are reviewed when changes occur
