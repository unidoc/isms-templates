# CC5.3: Deploys control activities through policies and procedures

## Purpose

The entity deploys control activities through policies that establish what is expected
and procedures that put policies into action.

## Description

Policies define the organization's requirements and expectations for controls. Procedures
operationalize those policies into actionable steps. Both are documented, communicated,
reviewed periodically, and updated as needed. Personnel are trained on relevant policies
and procedures.

## Key Considerations

- Policies are formally approved and communicated
- Procedures document step-by-step implementation of policies
- Policies and procedures are reviewed at least annually
- Personnel acknowledge and are trained on relevant policies
- Version control and change tracking are maintained
