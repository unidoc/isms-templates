# CC5.2: Selects and develops general controls over technology

## Purpose

The entity also selects and develops general control activities over technology to support
the achievement of objectives.

## Description

The organization implements IT general controls (ITGCs) that underpin the reliable
operation of technology. ITGCs include controls over access management, change management,
IT operations, and system development lifecycle. These controls provide the foundation
for application-level controls.

## Key Considerations

- IT general controls are documented and maintained
- Controls cover access management, change management, operations, and SDLC
- ITGCs are tested for operating effectiveness
- Dependencies between ITGCs and application controls are understood
