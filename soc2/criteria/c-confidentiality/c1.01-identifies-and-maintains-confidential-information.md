# C1.1: Identification and maintenance of confidential information

## Purpose

The entity identifies and maintains confidential information to meet the entity's
objectives related to confidentiality.

## Description

The organization identifies what information is considered confidential through a data
classification scheme. Confidential information is inventoried, labelled, and protected
according to its classification. The lifecycle of confidential information is managed
from creation through disposal.

## Key Considerations

- Data classification scheme is defined and applied
- Confidential information is inventoried
- Labelling and handling requirements are defined per classification
- Data owners are assigned for confidential information
- Classification is reviewed when the nature of information changes
