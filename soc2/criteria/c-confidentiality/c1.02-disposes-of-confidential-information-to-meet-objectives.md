# C1.2: Disposal of confidential information

## Purpose

The entity disposes of confidential information to meet the entity's objectives
related to confidentiality.

## Description

The organization implements secure disposal procedures for confidential information
when it is no longer needed. Disposal methods are appropriate to the sensitivity of
the information and the media on which it is stored. Disposal is documented and
verified.

## Key Considerations

- Disposal methods are defined for each media type
- Disposal is appropriate to the data classification
- Certificates of destruction are obtained for sensitive data
- Electronic media is securely wiped or destroyed
- Physical documents are shredded or incinerated
- Cloud data is securely deleted with verification
