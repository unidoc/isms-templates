# CC6.7: Restriction of data transmission, movement, and removal

## Purpose

The entity restricts the transmission, movement, and removal of information to authorized
internal and external users and processes, and protects it during transmission, movement,
or removal to meet the entity's objectives.

## Description

The organization controls data flows to ensure that information is transmitted only through
authorized channels, protected by encryption in transit, and subject to data loss prevention
controls. Removable media usage is restricted and monitored.

## Key Considerations

- Encryption is required for data in transit (TLS 1.2+)
- Data loss prevention (DLP) controls are implemented
- Removable media usage is restricted by policy
- Email and file transfer controls are in place
- Data classification drives transmission controls
