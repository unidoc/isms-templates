# CC6.3: Role-based access and least privilege

## Purpose

The entity authorizes, modifies, or removes access to data, software, functions, and other
protected information assets based on roles, responsibilities, or the system design and
changes, giving consideration to the concepts of least privilege and segregation of duties.

## Description

The organization implements role-based access control (RBAC) that maps user roles to the
minimum access required to perform their duties. Access changes are triggered by role changes,
transfers, or terminations. Periodic access reviews validate that access remains appropriate.

## Key Considerations

- Role-based access control (RBAC) is implemented
- Access is granted based on the principle of least privilege
- Segregation of duties is enforced for critical functions
- Access reviews are conducted periodically (at least quarterly for privileged access)
- Access is promptly revoked upon termination or role change
