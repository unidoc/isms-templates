# CC4.1: Ongoing and/or separate evaluations

## Purpose

The entity selects, develops, and performs ongoing and/or separate evaluations to ascertain
whether the components of internal control are present and functioning.

## Description

The organization implements monitoring activities that include ongoing evaluations
(embedded in business processes) and separate evaluations (internal audits, penetration
tests, vulnerability scans). The scope and frequency of evaluations are based on risk.

## Key Considerations

- Continuous monitoring tools are deployed (SIEM, alerting, dashboards)
- Internal audits are conducted on a risk-based schedule
- Vulnerability assessments and penetration tests are performed regularly
- Control effectiveness is measured through key metrics
- Evaluation results are documented and tracked
