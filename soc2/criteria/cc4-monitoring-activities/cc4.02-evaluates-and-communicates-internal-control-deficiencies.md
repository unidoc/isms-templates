# CC4.2: Evaluates and communicates internal control deficiencies

## Purpose

The entity evaluates and communicates internal control deficiencies in a timely manner
to those parties responsible for taking corrective action, including senior management
and the board of directors, as appropriate.

## Description

Identified control deficiencies are assessed for severity, reported to appropriate
stakeholders, and tracked through remediation. Deficiencies that represent significant
issues are escalated to senior management and the board.

## Key Considerations

- Process for reporting control deficiencies to management
- Severity classification of deficiencies
- Timely escalation of significant deficiencies
- Tracking and closure of remediation actions
- Lessons learned are incorporated into control improvements
