# P2.1: Choice and consent

## Purpose

The entity communicates choices available to data subjects regarding the collection, use,
retention, disclosure, and disposal of personal information and obtains consent.

## Description

The organization informs data subjects of choices available regarding their personal
information and obtains consent for the collection and processing of that information.
Consent mechanisms are appropriate to the sensitivity of the data and applicable
legal requirements.

## Key Considerations

- Consent mechanisms are implemented (opt-in, opt-out as appropriate)
- Consent records are maintained
- Data subjects can modify or withdraw consent
- Consent requirements comply with applicable privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA)
- Implicit vs. explicit consent requirements are determined by data sensitivity
