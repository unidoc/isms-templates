# P8.1: Privacy complaint management

## Purpose

The entity implements a process for receiving, addressing, resolving, and communicating
the resolution of inquiries, complaints, and disputes from data subjects and others and
periodically monitors compliance.

## Description

The organization maintains a privacy complaint and inquiry process that allows data
subjects and other parties to raise concerns about privacy practices. Complaints are
investigated, resolved, and documented. Trends are analysed to identify systemic issues.

## Key Considerations

- Privacy complaint submission mechanism is accessible
- Complaints are acknowledged, investigated, and resolved
- Resolution timeframes are defined and monitored
- Complaint trends are analysed for systemic issues
- Escalation paths are defined for unresolved complaints
- Compliance with privacy obligations is periodically monitored
