# P7.1: Quality of personal information

## Purpose

The entity collects and maintains accurate, up-to-date, complete, and relevant personal
information for the purposes identified in the notice.

## Description

The organization implements data quality controls to ensure that personal information
is accurate, complete, and current. Processes exist for data subjects to update their
information, and periodic data quality reviews are conducted.

## Key Considerations

- Data quality controls are implemented at the point of collection
- Data subjects can update their information
- Periodic data quality reviews are conducted
- Inaccurate data is corrected or deleted
- Data quality metrics are tracked
