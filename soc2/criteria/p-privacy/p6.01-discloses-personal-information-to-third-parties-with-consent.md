# P6.1: Disclosure of personal information to third parties

## Purpose

The entity discloses personal information to third parties with the consent of the data
subjects and only for the purposes identified in the privacy notice.

## Description

The organization controls the disclosure of personal information to third parties,
ensuring that disclosures are authorized, consistent with the privacy notice, and
subject to appropriate contractual protections. Data processing agreements are
executed with third parties that receive personal information.

## Key Considerations

- Third-party data sharing is authorized and documented
- Data processing agreements are in place with all recipients
- Disclosures are consistent with the privacy notice
- Cross-border transfers comply with applicable regulations
- Third-party compliance is monitored
