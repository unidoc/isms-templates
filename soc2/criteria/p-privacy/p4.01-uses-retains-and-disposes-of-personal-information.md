# P4.1: Use, retention, and disposal of personal information

## Purpose

The entity limits the use of personal information to the purposes identified in the
privacy notice and for which consent was obtained. Personal information is retained
for no longer than necessary, and disposed of securely.

## Description

The organization uses personal information only for the purposes identified in the
privacy notice. Retention periods are defined for each category of personal information,
and data is securely disposed of when no longer needed. Use of personal information
for new purposes requires additional consent.

## Key Considerations

- Use of personal information is limited to stated purposes
- Retention schedules are defined and enforced
- Secure disposal procedures are implemented
- Secondary use requires additional consent
- Retention and disposal are documented and auditable
