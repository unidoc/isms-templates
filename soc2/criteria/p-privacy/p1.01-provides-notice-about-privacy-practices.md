# P1.1: Privacy notice

## Purpose

The entity provides notice to data subjects about its privacy practices to meet the
entity's objectives related to privacy.

## Description

The organization provides clear, conspicuous, and accessible privacy notices to data
subjects that describe what personal information is collected, how it is used, with
whom it is shared, and what choices data subjects have regarding their information.

## Key Considerations

- Privacy notice is published and accessible to data subjects
- Notice covers collection, use, disclosure, and retention
- Notice describes data subject rights and how to exercise them
- Notice is updated when practices change
- Notice is provided at or before the point of collection
