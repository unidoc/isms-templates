# CC3.3: Considers fraud risk

## Purpose

The entity considers the potential for fraud in assessing risks to the achievement
of objectives.

## Description

The organization evaluates various types of fraud, including fraudulent reporting,
misappropriation of assets, unauthorized access to data, and corruption. The assessment
considers incentives and pressures, opportunities, attitudes and rationalizations that
may lead to fraudulent activity.

## Key Considerations

- Fraud risk assessment is part of the overall risk assessment process
- Types of fraud considered: reporting fraud, data theft, asset misappropriation
- Assessment considers management override of controls
- Anti-fraud controls are implemented based on identified risks
