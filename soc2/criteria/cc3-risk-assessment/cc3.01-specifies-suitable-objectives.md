# CC3.1: Specifies suitable objectives

## Purpose

The entity specifies objectives with sufficient clarity to enable the identification and
assessment of risks relating to objectives.

## Description

The organization defines objectives for security, availability, processing integrity,
confidentiality, and privacy that are specific, measurable, and aligned with the Trust
Services Criteria. Objectives consider the nature of the entity's business, regulatory
requirements, and stakeholder expectations.

## Key Considerations

- Security objectives are formally documented
- Objectives align with business strategy and stakeholder needs
- Objectives consider applicable regulations and contractual commitments
- Objectives are reviewed and updated periodically
