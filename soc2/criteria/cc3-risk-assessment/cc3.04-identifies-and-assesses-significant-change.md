# CC3.4: Identifies and assesses significant change

## Purpose

The entity identifies and assesses changes that could significantly impact the system
of internal control.

## Description

The organization monitors for changes in the external environment (regulatory, technological,
economic) and internal environment (new systems, restructuring, personnel changes) that could
affect the control environment. Significant changes trigger a reassessment of risks and controls.

## Key Considerations

- Process exists to identify significant internal and external changes
- Changes to technology, regulations, and business operations are monitored
- Impact of significant changes on controls is assessed
- Controls are updated in response to significant changes
