# CC3.2: Identifies and analyzes risks

## Purpose

The entity identifies risks to the achievement of its objectives across the entity and
analyzes risks as a basis for determining how the risks should be managed.

## Description

The organization performs a comprehensive risk assessment that identifies threats and
vulnerabilities to the system, evaluates the likelihood and impact of those risks, and
prioritizes risks for treatment. Risk identification considers both internal and external
factors, including changes in the operating environment.

## Key Considerations

- Risk identification covers all in-scope systems and processes
- Threat and vulnerability assessments are conducted
- Likelihood and impact are evaluated using a defined methodology
- Risks are documented in a risk register
- Risk assessment is performed at least annually
