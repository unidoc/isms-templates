# A1.3: Recovery plan testing

## Purpose

The entity tests recovery plan procedures supporting system recovery to meet its objectives.

## Description

The organization regularly tests its disaster recovery and business continuity plans to
validate that recovery objectives (RTO and RPO) can be met. Testing includes tabletop
exercises, functional tests of backup restoration, and full-scale disaster recovery
simulations. Test results are documented and plans are updated based on findings.

## Key Considerations

- Recovery plans are tested at least annually
- Testing includes backup restoration validation
- Recovery time objectives (RTO) are validated
- Recovery point objectives (RPO) are validated
- Test results are documented and deficiencies addressed
- Plans are updated based on test findings
