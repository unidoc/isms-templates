# A1.1: Capacity management and availability monitoring

## Purpose

The entity maintains, monitors, and evaluates current processing capacity and use of
system components (infrastructure, data, and software) to manage capacity demand and to
enable the implementation of additional capacity to help meet its objectives.

## Description

The organization monitors system capacity and performance metrics to ensure that processing
demands can be met. Capacity planning considers current utilization, growth trends, and
peak demand scenarios. Thresholds and alerts are configured to identify capacity issues
before they impact availability.

## Key Considerations

- System capacity is monitored in real time
- Capacity planning considers growth and peak demand
- Alerts are configured for capacity thresholds
- Auto-scaling is implemented where appropriate
- Capacity plans are reviewed at least annually
