# A1.2: Environmental protections, software, processes, and recovery infrastructure

## Purpose

The entity authorizes, designs, develops or acquires, implements, operates, approves,
maintains, and monitors environmental protections, software, data backup processes, and
recovery infrastructure to meet its objectives.

## Description

The organization implements environmental and infrastructure controls to ensure system
availability. This includes redundant infrastructure, data backup and restoration,
disaster recovery capabilities, and environmental controls (power, cooling, fire
suppression) for physical facilities.

## Key Considerations

- Data backup procedures are defined and automated
- Backup integrity is verified regularly
- Recovery infrastructure is maintained (hot/warm/cold sites)
- Environmental controls protect physical infrastructure
- Redundancy is implemented for critical components
- Disaster recovery procedures are documented
