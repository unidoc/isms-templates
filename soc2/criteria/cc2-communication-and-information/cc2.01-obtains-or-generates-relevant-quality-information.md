# CC2.1: Obtains or generates relevant, quality information

## Purpose

The entity obtains or generates and uses relevant, quality information to support the
functioning of internal control.

## Description

The organization identifies information requirements, captures data from internal and
external sources, and processes it into quality information that supports decision-making
and control activities. Information must be sufficient, timely, current, accurate, and
accessible to those who need it.

## Key Considerations

- Information requirements are identified for each control activity
- Data sources are evaluated for reliability and completeness
- Information processing maintains data integrity
- Data quality monitoring and correction processes exist
