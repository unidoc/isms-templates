# CC2.2: Internal communication of objectives and responsibilities

## Purpose

The entity internally communicates information, including objectives and responsibilities
for internal control, necessary to support the functioning of internal control.

## Description

The organization communicates policies, procedures, objectives, and individual
responsibilities through appropriate channels. This includes security awareness
programmes, policy distribution, and regular communication of changes to the
control environment.

## Key Considerations

- Policies and procedures are communicated to all relevant personnel
- Security awareness training is conducted regularly
- Changes to controls or responsibilities are communicated promptly
- Communication channels enable upward reporting (e.g., whistleblowing)
