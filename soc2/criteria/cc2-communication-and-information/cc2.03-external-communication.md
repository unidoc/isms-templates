# CC2.3: External communication

## Purpose

The entity communicates with external parties regarding matters affecting the functioning
of internal control.

## Description

The organization establishes processes to communicate relevant information to customers,
vendors, regulators, and other external parties. This includes system descriptions,
commitments, service level agreements, incident notifications, and regulatory reporting.

## Key Considerations

- External communication policies define what, when, and to whom
- Customer commitments (SLAs, contracts) are documented and communicated
- Incident notification procedures are defined for external parties
- Regulatory reporting obligations are identified and met
