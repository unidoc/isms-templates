# CC1.1: Commitment to integrity and ethical values

## Purpose

The entity demonstrates a commitment to integrity and ethical values.

## Description

The board of directors and management, at all levels, demonstrate through their directives,
actions, and behaviour the importance of integrity and ethical values to support the functioning
of the system of internal control. This includes establishing standards of conduct, evaluating
adherence, and addressing deviations in a timely manner.

## Key Considerations

- Code of conduct or ethics policy exists and is communicated
- Tone at the top reinforces ethical behaviour
- Deviations from expected standards are identified and remediated
- Third-party relationships are evaluated for ethical alignment
