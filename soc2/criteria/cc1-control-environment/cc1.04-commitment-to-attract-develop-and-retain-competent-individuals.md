# CC1.4: Commitment to attract, develop, and retain competent individuals

## Purpose

The entity demonstrates a commitment to attract, develop, and retain competent individuals
in alignment with objectives.

## Description

The organization establishes policies and practices for recruiting, training, evaluating,
and retaining personnel with competencies aligned to their responsibilities. This includes
succession planning for key roles and ongoing professional development.

## Key Considerations

- Job descriptions define required competencies
- Hiring processes include background checks where appropriate
- Training and development programmes are in place
- Performance evaluations are conducted regularly
- Succession planning for critical roles
