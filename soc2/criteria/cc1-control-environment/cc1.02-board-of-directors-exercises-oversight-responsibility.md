# CC1.2: Board of directors exercises oversight responsibility

## Purpose

The board of directors demonstrates independence from management and exercises oversight of
the development and performance of internal control.

## Description

The board (or equivalent oversight body) retains oversight responsibility for the design,
implementation, and operating effectiveness of controls. The board evaluates management's
ability to meet its obligations and holds management accountable.

## Key Considerations

- Board or oversight committee charter defines responsibilities
- Board members possess relevant expertise
- Regular meetings with documented minutes
- Independence from management is maintained
