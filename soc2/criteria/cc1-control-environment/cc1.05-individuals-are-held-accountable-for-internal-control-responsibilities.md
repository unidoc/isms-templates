# CC1.5: Individuals are held accountable for internal control responsibilities

## Purpose

The entity holds individuals accountable for their internal control responsibilities
in the pursuit of objectives.

## Description

The organization establishes mechanisms to communicate internal control responsibilities,
measures performance against those responsibilities, and takes corrective action when
individuals do not fulfil their duties. Accountability is enforced through performance
metrics, disciplinary policies, and incentive structures.

## Key Considerations

- Internal control responsibilities are defined and communicated
- Performance metrics include security and compliance objectives
- Corrective and disciplinary actions are defined for non-compliance
- Incentive structures do not encourage unethical behaviour
