# CC1.3: Management establishes structure, authority, and responsibility

## Purpose

Management establishes, with board oversight, structures, reporting lines, and appropriate
authorities and responsibilities in the pursuit of objectives.

## Description

The organization defines reporting structures, assigns authority for key functions,
and establishes clear lines of responsibility. This includes the definition of roles
for information security, technology operations, compliance, and risk management.

## Key Considerations

- Organizational chart with clear reporting lines
- Defined roles and responsibilities for security and compliance
- Delegation of authority is documented
- Segregation of duties is considered
