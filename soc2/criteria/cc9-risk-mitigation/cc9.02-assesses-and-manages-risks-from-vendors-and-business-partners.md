# CC9.2: Vendor and business partner risk management

## Purpose

The entity assesses and manages risks associated with vendors and business partners.

## Description

The organization evaluates risks arising from third-party relationships including vendors,
service providers, and business partners. Due diligence is performed prior to engagement,
contracts include security requirements, and ongoing monitoring ensures continued compliance.
The entity considers the impact of vendor dependencies on its ability to meet commitments.

## Key Considerations

- Vendor risk assessment is performed before engagement
- Contracts include security, privacy, and compliance requirements
- Vendor SOC reports or equivalent assurances are obtained and reviewed
- Ongoing monitoring of vendor compliance is performed
- Vendor inventory is maintained and classified by criticality
- Subcontractor and fourth-party risks are considered
