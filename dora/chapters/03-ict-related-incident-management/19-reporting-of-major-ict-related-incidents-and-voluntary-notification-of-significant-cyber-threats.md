# Article 19: Reporting of major ICT-related incidents

## Purpose

Report major ICT-related incidents to the relevant competent authority using initial, intermediate, and final notification stages.

## Reporting Timeline

| Stage | Deadline |
|-------|----------|
| Initial notification | Within 4 hours of classification / 24 hours of detection |
| Intermediate report | Within 72 hours of initial notification |
| Final report | Within 1 month of incident |
