# Article 26: Advanced testing (TLPT)

## Purpose

Financial entities identified by competent authorities shall carry out threat-led penetration testing (TLPT) at least every 3 years. TLPT shall cover critical or important functions and be performed on live production systems.

## TLPT Requirements

- Cover critical/important functions
- Performed on live production systems
- Use external testers (with limited exceptions)
- Follow TIBER-EU or equivalent framework
- At least every 3 years
