# Article 9: Protection and prevention

## Purpose

Continuously monitor and control the security and functioning of ICT systems. Minimise ICT risk through deployment of appropriate ICT security tools, policies, and procedures.

## Key Measures

- ICT security policies
- Strong authentication mechanisms
- Encryption
- Automated mechanisms to isolate affected information assets
- Network security management
