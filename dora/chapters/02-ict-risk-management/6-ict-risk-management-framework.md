# Article 6: ICT risk management framework

## Purpose

Establish and maintain a sound, comprehensive, and well-documented ICT risk management framework as part of the overall risk management system.

## Framework Components

- Strategies, policies, procedures, ICT protocols, and tools
- Mechanisms to identify, classify, and document ICT risks
- Annual reviews and audits by internal audit
