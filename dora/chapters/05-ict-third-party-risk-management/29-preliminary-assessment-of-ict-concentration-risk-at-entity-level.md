# Article 29: Preliminary assessment of ICT concentration risk

## Purpose

Before entering into a contractual arrangement for critical/important functions, assess concentration risk including from sub-outsourcing. Assess whether the arrangement would lead to excessive dependence on a single ICT third-party.
