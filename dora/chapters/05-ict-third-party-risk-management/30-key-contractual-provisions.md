# Article 30: Key contractual provisions

## Purpose

Contractual arrangements for ICT services shall contain mandatory provisions covering service level descriptions, data handling, access rights, termination, audit rights, and exit strategies.

## Required Contractual Elements

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Service levels | Clear descriptions with quantitative and qualitative targets |
| Data location | Processing and storage locations; notification of changes |
| Availability | Requirements for availability, authenticity, integrity, confidentiality |
| Termination | Adequate notice periods and transition assistance |
| Audit rights | Unrestricted rights of access, inspection, and audit |
| Exit strategy | Adequate transition periods and support for orderly exit |
