# Article 28: General principles

## Purpose

Financial entities shall manage ICT third-party risk as an integral component of ICT risk within the ICT risk management framework. They remain fully responsible for compliance.

## Key Obligations

- Maintain a register of all ICT third-party contractual arrangements
- Report annually to competent authorities on new arrangements for critical/important functions
- Consider risks from sub-outsourcing and concentration
