# Article 45: Information-sharing arrangements

## Purpose

Financial entities may exchange amongst themselves cyber threat information and intelligence, including indicators of compromise, tactics, techniques, and procedures, and cyber security alerts and configuration tools.

## Key Requirements

- Participation must be within trusted communities
- Protect personal data and business confidentiality
- Notify competent authorities of participation
- Implement safeguards for shared information
