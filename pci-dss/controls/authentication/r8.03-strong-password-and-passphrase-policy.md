# Strong password and passphrase policy

Implement strong password/passphrase policy requiring minimum 12 characters (or 8 if system cannot support 12), complexity, no reuse of last 4 passwords, and change at least every 90 days. Lock accounts after 10 invalid attempts for at least 30 minutes.
