# 5.1: Leadership and commitment

Top management shall demonstrate leadership and commitment with respect to the AIMS, including:

- Ensuring AI policy and objectives are established
- Ensuring integration of AIMS requirements into business processes
- Ensuring resources are available
- Communicating the importance of responsible AI
- Ensuring intended outcomes are achieved
- Directing and supporting continual improvement
