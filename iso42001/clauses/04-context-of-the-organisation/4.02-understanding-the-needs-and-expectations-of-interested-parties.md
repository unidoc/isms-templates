# 4.2: Interested parties

| Party | Requirements | How addressed |
|-------|-------------|---------------|

| Customers | Transparency, fairness, reliability of AI outputs | |
| Employees | AI impact on work, retraining, augmentation vs replacement | |

| Society | Ethical AI, non-discrimination, environmental impact | |
| Partners/suppliers | AI supply chain transparency, model provenance | |
