# 6.1: Actions to address risks and opportunities

## 6.1.1 General

Determine risks and opportunities considering the context, interested parties, and AI-specific concerns.

## 6.1.2 AI risk assessment

Perform AI-specific risk assessments considering:
- Risks to individuals (discrimination, privacy, safety, autonomy)
- Risks to groups and society (bias amplification, job displacement, environmental impact)
- Risks to the organization (liability, reputation, regulatory non-compliance)
- Technical risks (model drift, data quality, adversarial attacks, hallucination)
