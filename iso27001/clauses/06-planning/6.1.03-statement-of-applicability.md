# 6.1.3: Statement of Applicability

## Purpose

The Statement of Applicability (SoA) documents which Annex A controls are applicable and their implementation status.

## Generating the SoA

Run `isms soa generate` to build the SoA from policy and risk control references.
Run `isms soa gaps` to identify controls with no evidence.
