# 6.1: Risk assessment process

## Purpose

Define the information security risk assessment process.

## Risk Methodology

This ISMS uses a 5x5 likelihood x impact matrix. See `isms risk matrix` for the current matrix.

## Risk Criteria

| Score | Level    | Action Required              |
|-------|----------|------------------------------|
| 1-4   | Low      | Accept, monitor              |
| 5-9   | Medium   | Treat within planned cycle   |
| 10-15 | High     | Treat within 90 days         |
| 16-25 | Critical | Immediate action required    |
